#### [MiniGPT-4 ](<https://github.com/Vision-CAIR/MiniGPT-4>)

围绕图片进行对话

#### 特性

上传一张图片，可以问图片里的内容，围绕着图片进行对话， `MiniGPT-4` 会给出很多图片上的内容，而且这款可以支持在线测试。

如我上传了一张蓝猫的照片，问他这是什么动物，她是能回答正确的。

> This is a British Shorthair cat.

我们在让他根据图片讲一个故事，

> The British Shorthair is a breed of domestic cat that originated in the United Kingdom in the late 1940s. They are known for their short, plush coats and distinctive blue eyes. They are a medium-sized cat, with a muscular, athletic build and a round head. Their coats are thick and plush, with a dense undercoat that provides warmth and protection against the elements. They come in a variety of colors, including blue, black, brown, and silver. British Shorthairs are known for their gentle, affectionate personalities and are often described as being lap cats. They are also intelligent and playful, making them a popular choice as a family pet.

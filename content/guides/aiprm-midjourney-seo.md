#### 特性

这是一款插件，[google插件](<https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/aiprm-for-chatgpt/ojnbohmppadfgpejeebfnmnknjdlckgj?hl=en>) ,首先你需要一个chatgpt的账号。

AIPRM 能帮我们做很多的事情，如midjourney 提示词，SEO优化，小说，视频脚本等等。

我们用 arprm 来生成midjourney 提示词

> Cute Robots Dancing in the Snow

他会生成如下的几个提示词：


Prompt 1:
Cute robot, dancing in the snow, cartoon illustration style, playful and whimsical, watercolorand ink, Illustration by Hayao Miyazaki and James Gurney


Prompt 2:
Cute robot, dancing in the snow, hyper-realistic, a high-key image with a lot of snow in thebackground, shot with a 50mm lens, no artist


Prompt 3:
Cute robot, dancing in the snow, futuristic, neon, cyberpunk, stylized, 3D renderingIllustration by Syd Mead and John Park


Prompt 4:
Cute robot, dancing in the snow, low poly, polygonal, 3D animation, minimalistic, geometric,Illustration by Joshua Davis and Zach Lieberman.
